Family Solicitors London

After the break down of a relationship it can be a very emotional and stressful situation. Aletta Shaw Solicitors can offer practical solutions to any problems you might encounter and can offer legal advice and support through the legal process.

We offer video meetings on Skype, so you can have a face to face meeting without having to leave work or home.

It is very important to seek independent legal advice as early as possible to prevent you taking actions that could later prejudice your position.
