Why Choose Us?

We give proactive advice which will give you the best possible chance of avoiding a tribunal claim and employee dissatisfaction.

If a tribunal claim has been made or is unavoidable we will deal with the claim in a cost effective manner and argue your case robustly.

We represent companies in a range of claims from unfair dismissal to complex TUPE. Although it is very unusual to be awarded costs in tribunal proceedings, we have secured costs awards for our clients on a number of occasions.
