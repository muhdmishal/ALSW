Efe Ejaife

Solicitor/Head of Conveyancing

Efe qualified as a Solicitor in March 2010 and joined Aletta Shaw in July 2014, having previously worked for other London and local law firms.

Efe is the Head of the Property team and specialises in varied transactions involving residential and commercial property.

Efe takes great pride in her work where attention to detail is essential. During her time at Aletta Shaw thus far, Efe has established excellent rapport with her clients and other professionals whilst ensuring each transaction is dealt with as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Hobbies and Interests

In her spare time, Efe enjoys reading, arts, theatre and travelling as well as socialising with family and friends.
