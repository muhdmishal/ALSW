Corporate/Commercial Contracts

Well drafted and properly executed contractual documents are vital for a business to operate effectively. Poorly drafted legal documents, or the absence of documents, can lead to unnecessary risks and costly disputes which can be damaging to a business’s reputation. Parties can be reluctant to negotiate sensitive terms for fear of harming a fruitful business relationship. Instructing solicitors can help achieve a favourable arm’s length deal while preserving internal and external business relationships for clients.

Often businesses and individuals are presented with pre-drafted terms and conditions (by a supplier or financier for example) which can be unfairly one sided. Instructing solicitors can help identify and remove onerous terms resulting in a more balanced agreement. We can review standard documents or draft from scratch for a one off fixed fee.

  • Terms and conditions.
  • Shareholder agreements and partnership agreements.
  • Licencing and distribution agreements.
  • E-commerce and internet trading.
  • IT agreements including software support and hardware maintenance.
  • Employment contracts and staff handbooks.
  • Company articles of association.
  • Joint venture agreements between businesses and individuals.
  • Financial documents including loan agreements and guarantee agreements.
  • Mortgages and charges.
  • Franchise and outsourcing agreements.
  • Director service agreements.
