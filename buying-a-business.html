Buying/Selling a Business

Buying or selling a business can be a stressful and uncertain time with many hurdles and pit falls to avoid. Whether a buyer or a seller, our team is on hand with expertise in all corporate deals from international multi-million pound share sales to smaller high street asset transfers.

Every sale is unique but first class lawyers should be a standard on all transactions irrespective of the purchase price. In any sale or asset transfer there will always be a risk of assuming unforeseen liabilities and costs. It is essential that buyers and sellers receive proper legal advice in order to achieve the best deal for them and to ensure legal documents are watertight. Where necessary our team can advise on commercial property and on tax and accounting through experienced third parties.

At Aletta Shaw we believe in building a strong working relationship with our clients. We also believe in effective dealing with lawyers for other parties to ensure our transactions complete efficiently and within budget.
