Michael Baggott

Family Team

Michael has been working in the legal sector for over 12 years. He first joined a local law firm as an office junior back in 2004 and was then promoted to paralegal and worked in the debt recovery department where he gained expertise in debt recovery and worked with a number of well-known commercial companies. Michael then moved to a firm in Bromley in Kent where he dealt with RTA and Credit Hire litigation and progressed to becoming a senior paralegal. Michael was then employed as a Department Manager for large firm of solicitors that are based near Brighton and helped to expand the company into Fleet Street, London. Michael headed up the Subrogated Loss and Part 8 departments and also run the office as Office Manager of these premises. Michael worked with a number of top insurers and often recovered in excess of £1,000,000 for them per month. In 2014 Michael joined Aletta Shaw Solicitors as a Legal Assistant in the Family Department and gained considerable expertise in such areas as Divorce and Finances and has helped on a wide variety of other matters. Michael assists as an office manager and helps to maximise the efficiency of the office as Aletta Shaw expands its business year by year.

Michael has vast experience of Civil Law and Management of legal firms and is exceptional at client care and is always willing to go above and beyond to assist his client’s whether they are a private individual seeking assistance or a large multimillion pound operating company. Michael would be happy to discuss any matters with you.

Hobbies and Interests

Michael currently enjoys family time as he has recently become a Dad. In his spare time he enjoys playing football, squash and karate. Michael is a very keen motor enthusiast and often attends a number of car shows across the UK.
