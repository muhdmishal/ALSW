Company Secretary/Legal Compliance

There is no longer a statutory requirement for a company to have a company secretary, however directors continue to be liable for legal non-compliance and the penalties can be severe. In most cases we advise our clients that it is prudent to appoint a secretary to help protect the company and directors.
