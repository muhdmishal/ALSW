Property and Conveyancing

Quality

Aletta Shaw's dedicated property law team is based in our Bexleyheath office and provides conveyancing services to Kent and South East London. The team have expertise in residential and commercial conveyancing ranging from the sale of the family home to much larger corporate acquisitions. We pride ourselves in providing a highly professional service and a personal and tailored approach to client care.

At Aletta Shaw the person who will have primary responsibility for all your property work will be a solicitor or licensed conveyancer. This is not always the case at other legal practices. Clients of some firms are told that their work is being carried out by a 'lawyer'. Many people are not aware that the general term 'lawyer' does not necessarily mean that person actually holds any legal qualifications.
