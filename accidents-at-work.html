Accidents at Work Solicitors

Unfortunately, accidents do happen at work.

You may be suffering from repetitive strain injury because of a badly designed work station, or have slipped and tripped over cabling in the office.

We’ll be able to recover any medical costs or out-of-pocket expenses that you may have incurred, as well as damages for pain and suffering. You may even be entitled to damages for loss of earnings, if your injury meant that you couldn’t work.
