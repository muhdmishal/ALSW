Industrial Disease Claims

Often, we trust our employers to provide us with a safe workplace, and to take reasonable precautions to stop us becoming ill.

Unfortunately, that doesn’t always happen.

Every year, thousands of people develop industrial diseases through no fault of their own, ranging from asbestosis to vibration white finger, from pleural thickening to pneumoconiosis.

Here at Aletta Shaw, we can help you claim compensation, if you, a friend or a family member have an industrial disease, including:

  • Mesothelioma and Asbestos-related Illnesses
  • Noise-Induced Hearing Loss
  • Contact Dermatitis
  • Occupational Asthma
  • Vibration White Finger and Hand-Arm Vibration Syndrome

We’ll be able to recover any medical costs or out-of-pocket expenses that you may have incurred, as well as damages for pain and suffering. You may even be entitled to damages for loss of earnings, if your injury meant that you couldn’t work.
